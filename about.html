about us

"No one should feel unsafe while working on themselves" - David Spencer

"Gyms should be a safe space where people enjoy their time" - Jenny Spencer

Here at Spirit Gym we believe that everyone should feel comfortable and safe whilst working on themselves.

That's why we strive to provide the safest gym environment possible.

We are a family run business with a tight knit community that looks out for one another inside and out of the gym.

Our Story

Founded in 2012, our gym was set up by the Spencer siblings in Hitchin centre with the aim to create something special.

Within the first couple of years our gym had gained popularity becoming one of the most well known in all of Hitchin, allowing us to expand and modernize our facilities.

After this transition we turned our attention towards making our gym a socil environment where our members can relax. Therefore we created a small cafe in which members and non members can spend their time.
